Scalable & adaptable

Most large cities have up to 20% unutilized growing space in form of rooftops and balconies.

We analyse each situation individually, and adapt with as much or as little complexity as the situation demands

Our approach goes all the way from the small (balcony growing boxes) to the very large (rooftop farms).

Our rainwatercollection and self watering systems makes the maintainance low

If your crops do not grow as expected our concrete advice is just a quick text away

Active gardens

Our gardens go from the very small to the very large. It all depends on the space, conditions and budget available.

Regardless of size our gardens always produce food, save money and reduce transport.
Sometimes they also produce electricity, reduce sewage, create soil, collect water and grow fish.

It is all a happy mix of these systems:

- Grow boxes

- Rain water collection

- Self watering

- Solar panels

- Composting

- Nutrient solution recycling

- Aquaponics

Our system

The complete system below starts at a total cost of €200 - the max depends on your space, needs and own expertise level.
We make it happen in four steps:

1. Analysis

2. Setup

3. Quality Control

4. Community

1. Analysis

50€/20€

Quick visit to analyse:
- Your available space
- The conditions of your space: sun, wind, humidity
- Your wishes for the space: mix gardening/social
- First crop suggestions

The analysis can be made either through a personal visit, 50€ and approximately one hour, or (if you are more experienced with gardening) as a self assessment (20€) where we send you what info we need.

2. Set up

We set it up: >300€

- We bring the boxes, soil and plants

- We build the system

- We plant the first crop

- Usually takes under 2 hours, then you have a roof garden!

Self set up: >50€

- Materials, soil and plants are sent to your place

- You receive a manual for the setup

- You assemble the boxes yourself

- You plant your first crop as per manual

- You send us a couple for pictures for quality control

3. Quality Control

Making sure it works

Crop insurance

We want to make sure your systems works as expected and that you get what you pay for.
For 5€/month:

- We check in on your crops through pics

- We give concrete advice on actions

- If your system breaks we replace it

At the start of each new season we give you specific advice on which crop to plant next.

4. Community

Exchange crops and tips with your neighbours

Our food sharing app - free

Part of what we do is contributing to a circular economy. Through the community you can exchange crops with others, adding more variety to your plate.

- Find other urban farmers close to you

- Exchange crops

- Share growing tips

- Join common events
